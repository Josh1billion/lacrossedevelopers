About

This is a group that started with our first meeting on November 7, 2019. We are a laid-back group of programmers, entrepreneurs, tech enthusiasts, and game developers who hang out, discuss technology, and give presentations on topics of interest to each other. We welcome everyone, from experienced programmers, to students, to people who aren't into programming but enjoy technology.

Meeting Info

As of November 2023, meetings are held on the 4th Tuesday evening of every month at 6 PM at Inland Packaging (north visitor entrance @ 3209 Airport Rd, La Crosse, WI 54603).

If you have trouble getting into the building or need directions, please give a shout on the Discord. Meeting cancellations, reschedulings, etc. will also be posted there.

We also post meeting info on the calendar at https://that.us/communities/la-crosse-area-software-developers/
